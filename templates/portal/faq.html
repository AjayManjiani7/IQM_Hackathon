BRING CHANGE

FAQ'S

1) What is Bring Change?

BringChange is an innovative platform that uses technology to establish a relationship between citizens and the government for India's growth and development. The government hopes to promote citizen participation in good governance through this platform by soliciting ideas, suggestions, and grassroots contributions. BringChangeâ€™s objective is to empower citizens to work collaboratively with the government.

2) How Can I Be Part of Bring Change?

To participate, go to our Website and create a profile. You will be asked for personal information such as your name, contact information, and so on. You will also be asked to describe your skills and the issues on which you would like to contribute.

3) For Government Employee

So if you're a government employee with a @gov.in or @nic.in email address, you can use those credentials to log in without providing any other information.

4) For Public

The General public can register and sign up for BringChange using their 10-digit mobile number. When logging in, users can use either their registered mobile number or email. Once you enter your Mobile Number a One Time Password (OTP) will be sent to your registered mobile number. You do not need to remember any passwords to log in.

5) Why should I participate?

BringChange is a one-of-a-kind platform for citizen engagement via participatory governance. By registering with BringChange, you will be able to share your thoughts and ideas on issues of public concern through discussions, as well as directly participate in governance initiatives through the tasks earmarked on this portal to address issues of public concern. Most importantly, BringChange allows you to participate in various public-good initiatives and express your thoughts on the government's policy initiatives.

6) What are the benefits of participating?

Bring Change provides you with a readymade platform to connect with the government on a regular basis and contribute to policy formation and governance through its various features and initiatives. Credit-point-based incentives will be announced in the future.

7) How can I Send Feedbacks?

Any generic question about the Bring Change platform's content, design, service, or technology can be directed to this customised Feedback interface.

8) Facing problems regarding registration or log in?

If you have any questions or need assistance with the registration or login process, please contact us using this form. We would be delighted to respond and assist you in resolving any issues you may encounter while browsing and/or participating on BringChange, as we value your participation.

9) Any other issue

Despite the above - mentioned categories, if you are experiencing any other problems with the site, please do not hesitate to contact us. Let us know about your problem by providing a brief description of the problem. We would be delighted to get back to you.
